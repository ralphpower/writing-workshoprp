Everything you need to know

Do I need writing experience to attend? +

Not at all! This workshop is designed specifically for leaders who may have never written professionally. We focus on getting your ideas out, not on literary technique. If you can speak about your expertise, you can write about it.

What if I don't have a book idea yet? +

Perfect! Part of the workshop helps you identify your unique angle and core message. Many attendees come without a concrete idea and leave with a clear book concept.

How much time will I need to commit after the workshop? +

That depends on your goals, but most attendees dedicate 5-10 hours per week. We'll teach you strategies to write efficiently alongside your busy schedule.

Yes! All attendees receive 30-day access to the workshop recording so you can revisit key concepts and lessons.
